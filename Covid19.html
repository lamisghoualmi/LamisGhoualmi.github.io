Covid 19 dataset

Description of the dataset

Coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19) is a contagious disease caused by a virus, the severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 (SARS-CoV-2). The first known case was identified in Wuhan, China, in December 2019. The disease spread worldwide, leading to the COVID-19 pandemic. In this project, we are working on the coronavirus pandemic containing data from all around the world. The dataset is an open-source data obtained from Coronavirus (COVID-19)

Goal of my visualization

In this project, we are interested to visualize the total death per continent and per country from the first time that a Covid 19 case was discovered until April 2022
